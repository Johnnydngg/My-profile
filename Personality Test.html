Personality Test

Myers-briggs

Online personality and learning tests can provide a subtle outlook on how someone works in a team but also how they are as a person which can help businesses pick out potential employees who fit their description. The results from the Myers-Briggs personality test indicated that I had a campaigner personality, I would agree with this test as I’m someone who is curious and isn’t afraid to go out of my safe zone, I am also a festive person which means that I enjoy having deep and meaningful conversations with people. I also fit the weaknesses of a person who has a campaigner personality as I’m a people pleaser which means that I constantly care about how other people think about me and how I can please them, I’m also someone who is overly optimistic which makes it difficult for me to accept hard but necessary truths.

Education Planner and Auditory Test

The second test that I took was a learning test from education planner, it indicated to me that I’m an auditory learner. This meant that I’m someone who learns from hearing and listening which I can agree with as I find it easier to learn by listening. The Last test that I took was another personality test by PsychCentral which indicated that I was someone who enjoyed lively centre of attention, this outlined that people see me as someone whose fresh, lively, charming amusing and always interesting which I hope people do see me as these things.

Overview of these Test

These outcomes have indicated to me that I’m someone who enjoys working in a team and my behaviour about working in a team has not changed after seeing these results as I’m someone who puts in their best when other people are reliant on me. When scouting out potential teammates I need to find people who are similar to me, this comprises of people who enjoy working in a team and strives to do their best and not to disappoint their teammates.
